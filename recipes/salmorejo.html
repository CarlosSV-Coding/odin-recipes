Salmorejo

Description

Salmorejo is a traditional cold Spanish soup from CÃ³rdoba, made primarily from tomatoes, bread, olive oil, and garlic.

It's thick and creamy, often garnished with hard-boiled eggs and Spanish ham, making it a refreshing and hearty dish, especially popular in the hot summer months.

Ingredients (4 servings)

  • Tomatoes - 1 Kilogram
  • Telera or (French) Bread - 200 grams
  • Garlic - 1-2 clove(s)
  • (Extra virgin) Olive oil - 100ml
  • Salt - 10 grams
  • Hard-boiled eggs (optional)
  • Ham (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel the tomatoes' skin and cut in halves. Blend the tomatoes
  2. Remove the bread's crust and break in smaller pieces
  3. Peel the garlic clove(s)
  4. Add the olive oil and salt
  5. Blend all together until it is smooth and creamy
  6. Taste and add any of the ingredients according to your likings. You might want to add some bread or tomatoes depending on how thick or liquid you want it to be
  7. Serve cold with some hard-boiled eggs and ham on top. You can it eat with a spoon, as a sauce (e.g., with Spanish omelette) or dipping bread in it
