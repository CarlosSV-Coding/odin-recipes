Croquetas

Description

Croquetas are a popular Spanish tapa consisting of small, breaded, and fried rolls filled with a creamy mixture of béchamel sauce and finely chopped ingredients like ham, chicken, or cod. They're crispy on the outside and soft and flavorful on the inside, making them a beloved snack or appetizer.

Ingredients (6 servings)

    To prepare the béchamel filling we will need:

  • Serrano Ham - 200 grams
  • Milk - 1 litre
  • Flour - 110 grams
  • Butter - 110 grams
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt

    • To prepare the bread-coating we will need:

  • Eggs
  • Flour (optional)
  • Breadcrumbs (or panko)

Steps

  1. Melt the butter on a pan
  2. Chop up the ham in small cubes and cook it for 2 minutes
  3. Add the flour and mix
  4. Add the milk and cook at medium temperature. Keep whisking all the time until it becomes thick
  5. Use a wooden kitchen spoon and lower the temperature. Add salt and nutmeg
  6. Keep mixing with the spoon for 30 minutes
  7. Put the béchamel in a glass tray and cover it with food cling film (let it touch the surface of the béchamel so it does not create a crust on top)
  8. Let it cool down for 6 hours. Then put it in the fridge and let it rest overnight
  9. Take 3 plates: One with whisked eggs, one with flour, and one with breadcrumbs
  10. Use a spoon to grab a handful of béchamel
  11. Dip it in the eggs, then a give it a thin layer of flour, and then shape it as you give it a nice coat of breadcrumbs
  12. Keep doing the same thing until you finish bread-coating all the croquetas
  13. Deep fry in (extra virgin) olive oil at high temperature until the bread is toasty
  14. If you'd like to, you can freeze the unfried croquetas for another day
  15. Serve with your favorite sauce (e.g., ketchup, mayonnaise). Use your hands!!

Alternatives

  • You can add onion or any other vegetable to the mixture
  • You can use any other meat or vegetable instead of ham (e.g., chicken, prawns, mushrooms, tofu, etc.)
  • You can make it vegan with vegan butter and soy milk
