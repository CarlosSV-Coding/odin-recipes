Eggplants with honey

Description

Eggplants with honey are a traditional dish from CÃ³rdoba, Spain, consisting of thinly sliced, battered, and fried eggplant drizzled with honey or molasses. The combination of the crispy, savory eggplant and the sweet syrup creates a delightful contrast of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • Eggplants
  • Rice or corn flour
  • Beer
  • Honey or sugar molasses
  • (Extra virgin) Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash the eggplant
  2. Peel it and cut it in slices or in the shape of fries
  3. Put them in a bowl and salt them. Let them rest for 30 minutes
  4. Put them in a different (dry) bowl and pour some beer on them. Let them rest another 30 minutes
  5. Remove the beer and dry the eggplants with kitchen paper
  6. Apply a layer of flour
  7. Deep fry the eggplants in (extra virgin) olive oil at high temperature. It is better not to fry them all at once.
  8. Put them on a plate with kitchen paper and let them sweat the extra oil
  9. Serve them with honey or molasses
